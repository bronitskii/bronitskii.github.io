More about me:

As a highly skilled and passionate Translator / Interpreter, Photoshop Creator and Network Enthusiast, I offer a blend of language proficiency and technical skills. My experiences and skills include:

Languages:

Proficiency in Russian, English, and Ukrainian languages. I've successfully handled various translation projects across different fields including technical, IT, financial, educational, and gaming demonstrating my linguistic prowess. My experience encompasses a variety of translation projects: bilateral, legal documentation, website localisation, and multimedia translations. Furthermore, my work as an interpreter spans providing on-demand interpretation services for the NHS, emergency line 911, insurance companies, and social services.

Networking:

I am well versed in modern network security and encryption algorithms. My experience includes building and maintaining my home network, firewall configuration, managing virtual machines, managing mesh VPNs, cloud infrastructure administration. I use FIDO and PIV protocols for authentication and run various projects on my Raspberry Pi at home. I also provide remote technical support to my family members, demonstrating my ability to resolve network issues.

Photography:

My passion for photography has led me to develop a strong set of skills in photo editing with Adobe Photoshop. For my projects I use a Canon 7D Mk II camera along with a Sigma 17-50 mm lens. Whether working on personal or professional projects, I am always striving to achieve the best possible results by continuously refining my approach to photo editing and photographic techniques.
