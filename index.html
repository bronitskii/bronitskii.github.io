Languages:

Proficiency in Russian, English, and Ukrainian languages. I have successfully completed a number of translation projects across different fields, including technical, IT, financial, educational, and gaming demonstrating my linguistic prowess. My experience encompasses a variety of translation projects: bilateral, legal documentation, website localisation, and multimedia translations. Furthermore, my work as an interpreter spans providing on-demand interpretation services for the NHS, emergency line 911, insurance companies, and social services.

Networking:

I am well-versed in modern network security and encryption algorithms. My experience includes building and maintaining my home network and firewall configuration, managing virtual machines, managing mesh VPNs, cloud infrastructure administration. I use FIDO and PIV protocols for authentication and run various projects on my Raspberry Pi at home. I also provide remote technical support to my family members, demonstrating my ability to resolve various network issues.

Photography:

My passion for photography has led me to develop a strong set of skills in photo editing with Adobe Photoshop. For my projects I use both: a Canon 7D Mk II camera along with a Sigma 17-50 mm lens and iPhone 15 Pro. Whether working on personal or professional projects, I am always striving to achieve the best possible results by continuously refining my photo editing and photographic techniques.
